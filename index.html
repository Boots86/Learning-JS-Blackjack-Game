Blackjack

by Robert Buettner

Rules:

  • Aim for a score of 21, and try to beat the dealer's score without passing 21.
  • "Hit" to draw more cards and increase your score.
  • "Stay" to hold at your current score.
  • Dealer wins if they draw 5 or more cards with a score under 21.
  • Dealer will not draw cards with a score of over 16.
